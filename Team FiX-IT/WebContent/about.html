About Us

Hi,Welcome to our web app this is our team FiX-IT

Getting Vaccinated is a life saving hack.Everyone above 18+ have to take vaccine to get free from covid.Let me explain about how we get this idea.Suma is the friend she decided to get vaccine,but she don't know where is the vaccination center near by her.She decided to ask her friends and family members , but her they are all living in different places so they don't know about that.So how Suma will get out of this problem.Where can she find the details of the hospital.For this our team designed a web app where anyone can use it in order to find the details of the near by hospital,the charges on vaccine,opening and closing timings,type of vaccine giving all the things she can get upto date.We used public API which are available on the API SETU portal for extracting the details.The user can register for free in the portal before to see the details.Once he/she registered whenever they want,they can login and see the updated hospital details.
